About


B. Tech Facilities & Services Planning

B. Tech (FSP) programme is an interdisciplinary course and relates to design, planning, and maintenance of residential, commercial or institutional buildings, such as hospitals, clinics, hotels, resorts, schools, office complexes, convention centers. Subjects include the study of HVAC, electric power, firefighting, plumbing and lighting systems.

Further Studies 

  • Building Science Engineering (M-Tech)
  • Building Services Engineering
  • Environmental Geomatics
  • Fire Engineering
  • Construction Management
  • Water and Environmental technology
  • Sustainable Engineering
  • Facility Management
  • Health Safety and Environmental Management
  • Building Energy Performance
  • Energy & Environmental Management

The curriculum is structured for four years and is divided into eight semesters. The curriculum includes field visits and internship in professional organisation.

Career Opportunities:

This degree gives the student a variety of professional options. Both public and private sectors offer a range of jobs. Graduates can work as technical engineers in the fields of:

  • HVAC Engineer
  • Plumbing Engineer
  • Electrical Engineer
  • Engineer
  • Fire Engineer
  • MEP Engineer
  • MEP coordinator
  • Energy Analyst
  • Facility manager/Maintenance Engineer
  • Site Engineer for Services.
  • Safety Site Officer
  • MEP Modeler
  • Green Building Designer

Building Services engineers work closely with other construction professionals, architects, structural engineers and quantity surveyors. They influence the architecture of a building and play a significant role in the sustainability and energy demands of a building. Within building services engineering, new roles are emerging, in the areas of renewable energy, sustainability, low carbon technologies and energy management. With buildings accounting for around 50% of all carbon emissions, building services engineers play a significant role in combating climate change. As such, a typical building services engineer has a wide-ranging career path.

Eligibility: 

Intermediate (M.P.C) / 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry/equivalent qualification.

Selection procedure:

The students have to qualify in EAMCET conducted by the Government of Telangana.
