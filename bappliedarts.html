A 1985 batch IAS officer, Chitra Ramachandran currently is appointed Special Chief Secretary – Higher Education, Government of Telangana. She has been nominated by the Government as member of the Governing Body of Woxsen University. She holds a Master’s in political science from the University of Mumbai and was first appointed as assistant collector in the land revenue management and district administration department. She went on to work for other departments such as human resource development as deputy secretary deputy commissioner for finance department, managing director for consumer affairs, food and public distribution additional registrar for agriculture and cooperation, youth affairs and sports, tourism, transport, rural and urban development departments. She also is appointed Special Chief Secretary to the Government of Telengana(FAC) in the Housing Department.
